\section*{Research Interests}
% \indent My research interests lie in many aspects of condensed matter theory, particularly electronic systems with strong interaction. Rich and novel physics such as topological collective motion and fractionalization emerge in it. These collective degrees of freedom differ fundamentally from original electronic ones and can be observed in experimentally. I am interested in frustrated magnets, fractional quantum Hall systems, and high-Tc superconductors where such fascinating physics is believed to play a central role. And classification theory is demanded in grouping such topological phases. In spite of this, there are many strongly-correlated systems where no quasiparticle is well-defined. So transport theory is another field I am interested in. Apart from the surprisingly emergent structure like AdS/CMT correspondence, more importantly this field is tightly connected with experiments and can be directly examined.
